About

Welcome to Greencycle, where sustainability meets innovation. Nestled in the heart of Kaduna, opposite Flowermill on Nassarawa Bypass, Greencycle stands as a beacon of environmental responsibility and resource efficiency. At Greencycle, we are not just a company; we are a movement dedicated to reshaping the future of plastics. With a steadfast commitment to circularity, we harness the power of recycling to breathe new life into discarded materials. Our state-of-the-art facility utilizes cutting-edge technologies and processes to transform plastic waste into high-quality recycled plastics, paving the way for a cleaner, greener tomorrow. Driven by a deep sense of environmental stewardship, every member of the Greencycle team is united in our mission to reduce waste, conserve resources, and mitigate the impacts of plastic pollution. Through collaboration, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, we strive to redefine industry norms and set new standards for sustainability. Located strategically in Kaduna, opposite Flowermill on Nassarawa Bypass, Greencycle serves as a hub for sustainable practices, inspiring individuals and businesses alike to join us in our journey towards a more circular economy. As a leading supplier of recycled plastics, Greencycle is dedicated to meeting your specific demands and needs. Whether you require materials for packaging, manufacturing, or other applications, we can tailor our supply to accommodate your requirements. With flexibility and reliability at the core of our service, we are committed to providing you with the recycled plastics you need, when you need them. Join us at Greencycle, where we transform vision into action and plastics into possibilities. Together, let's create a world where waste becomes a valuable resource, and sustainability is the cornerstone of our future.
